About the Shelter



Dejan Gacic and his late mother Svetlana started feeding stray dogs in the village of Cumic, Serbia more than 20 years ago. Local residents noticed these small acts of kindnessâ€”Dejan and his mother developed a reputation for helping animals, and were asked by local residents to help a wolf that was being held in captivity.


The 'owner' of the wolf was about to release it into a canned hunt, where it would be sent running into the woods to be chased by hunters and their dogs. Dejan purchased the wolf for a single bottle of liquor, and hid the wolf in his basement. In 1997, Dejan and Svetlana established Vucjak Shelter. After his mother's passing, Dejan continued to run the shelter on his own. He started with a small shelter, however, over the years, the number of dogs has increased to more than a thousand animals.
